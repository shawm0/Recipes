The only wake-up call you need...

A premium coffee blend designed to give you the boost required for that all productive day ahead

Ingredients

  • Premium Coffee Pod - Flavour of Choice (Nespresso)
  • 1 Cup Full Cream Milk
  • 1/2 Cup Ice
  • 1 Scoop Ice Cream
  • Chocolate Powder

Method

  1. Put the pod into the coffee machine
  2. Place Glass under the outlet of the coffee machine
  3. Ensure there is enough water in the machine and press the start button on the coffee machine
  4. Once the coffee machine is finished, add the milk to the glass
  5. Add the ice to the glass of coffee
  6. Sprinkle the chocolate powder onto the coffee
