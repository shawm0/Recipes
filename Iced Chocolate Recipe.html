A Fresh Blend designed for those 40 Degree Summer Days.

A mix of all good things bringing you a freshly blended chocolate taste

Ingredients

  • Chocolate Syrup
  • 1 Cup Full Cream Milk
  • 1/2 Cup Ice
  • 2 Scoop Ice Cream
  • Chocolate Powder
  • 100g Melted Cadbury Milk Chocolate

Method

  1. In a jug, blend the milk, ice and melted chocolate together
  2. Line the glass with the chocolate syrup
  3. Add milk mixture to glass
  4. Add the 2 scoops of ice cream to the glass
  5. Sprinkle the chocolate powder over the top of the mixture
  6. Enjoy!
